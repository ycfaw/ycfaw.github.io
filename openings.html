Are you reading to start your journey to employment? Keep scrolling to find some websites that can help you find the right job for you!

General Job Sources

If you don't know what field you want to go in and want to look through a wide variety of jobs to decide, Snag A Job is the website for you. There are hundreds of thousands of job openings to choose from and range from full time positions to participation in studies.

Those who are looking for freelance work, remote work, and part-tme jobs should take at look at Flex Jobs. The site is free to join but paid members get access to advice and other benefits. Even those who don't have a paid membership get access to free news, job listings, and expert tips through the site's newsletter. Check out this site if you're looking for jobs with flexibility.

Want to build a freelance career? Visit UpWork. There are over a million companies looking to hire freelance workers on this site. Using UpWork allows you to find contract opportunities and you can also use the site to track your hours, billing, and more.

Seeing as it is one of the most recommended sites to use when looking for a job, Indeed should definitely be on your list of job source sites. With over 140 million visitors to the site every month, it caters to every individuals' needs. Because of it's massive influx of job openings, it's the perfect site for people just starting their job-hunting journey.

Glassdoor, although one of the smaller job boards with close to 6,000 listings, features one of the most important features: the ability to reserach companies. It also features vital job information, such as salary information and company reviews.

Monster is one of the most visited job boards out there. It hosts over a million job listings worldwide and allows users to upload resumes. You can take advantage of their career advice articles, resume services, and education partners.

When it comes to being the largest job board out there, Career Builder takes the cake. It operates in over 20 countries outside the U.S. and has 24 million visitors monthly. Search for jobs, post your resume, and save jobs you like so you can come back to them later.
